m365 (previously called Office 365) is a product family of productivity software, collaboration and cloud-based services owned by Microsoft.
It encompasses online services such as Outlook.com, OneDrive, Microsoft Teams, programs formerly marketed under the name Microsoft Office (including applications such as Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook on Microsoft Windows, macOS, mobile devices, and on the web), 
enterprise products and services associated with these products such as Exchange Server, SharePoint, and Viva Engage.  
m365 also covers subscription plans encompassing these products, including those that include subscription-based licenses to desktop and mobile software, and hosted email and intranet services.

identity management:microsoft Entra ID manages user identity and  sign-in forming the foundation of access control.
licensing and access:licensing determines which m365 services and applications a user can access.
core applications: applications like outlook,teams,onedrive and share point serve as tools built on identity and licensing.
data as real value:Emails,files, chats and documents represent the valuable data within m365 ecosystem.
built-in security and compliance: m365 includes security, compliance, and identity services to protect organisational data effectively.
